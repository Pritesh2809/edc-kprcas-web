Our Year-Round Activities

EDC-KPRCAS conducts practical and impactful events to help students become real entrepreneurs. From market stalls to investor pitches, every event is a step forward.

🧭 January: Student Food Venture Launch

4 student entrepreneurs launched a rent-free food venture in the canteen – our first real-world startup initiative.

💼 February: Meet with CEO

Hosted Mr. Mahesh Gopalakrishnan, CEO of Crayon’d, to mentor students on refining business ideas and networking strategies.

🌍 March: Cultural Fest Business Stalls

Students ran food stalls, gift shops, baking setups, and course sales — building business and communication skills.

📚 April: 2-Day IPR Workshop

Sessions on patents, trademarks, and business registration to build legal knowledge and startup readiness.

🎓 June: Freshers' Orientation

New students joined the cell without criteria — purely based on entrepreneurial interest. Team size: 50+ committed members.

🏭 July: Industrial Visit + Workshop

Visit to innovation hubs + workshop on identifying social problems and innovative solutions.

🛍️ August: Sell Your Product

Students created and sold products on campus to test market viability. Ended with reflective presentation sessions.

🧠 September: Design Thinking Bootcamp

Bootcamp on moving from ideation to prototyping with hands-on design thinking techniques.

⚙️ October: Hackathon + Prototype Building

One-month prototyping challenge ending in a hackathon judged by industry experts.

🎤 November: Investor Pitching Training

Experts trained students in pitch decks, confidence, and investment language. Ideas refined for final pitch.

🦈 December: Shark Tank Event

Top ideas pitched in front of judges and selected for incubation support. Winners entered the EDC pipeline for development.

Quarterly Stalls for Entrepreneurial Practice

Every quarter, we host on-campus stalls for students to apply real-world business skills. Focus: selling, communication, and resilience.
